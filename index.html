HOW TO PLAY

FIELD MANUAL — SIXTY SECONDS
THE MISSION Place defense systems between waves, then launch. Drones that reach the FOB cost integrity; at zero the base is overrun. Survive all 15 waves.
NOTHING SHOOTS WHAT NOTHING SEES Weapons only engage confirmed tracks. The FOB senses ~260m on its own; SENTINEL radar and ORACLE optics extend the picture. Fuzzy diamonds are unconfirmed contacts — get a sensor on them. RIDGES block line-of-sight to low flyers: radar shadows are attack corridors.
THE POWER GRID The FOB supplies 8 power and every system draws some. GENERATORS add +5. Nothing runs unpowered.
WIN THE EXCHANGE Ammunition costs money, rounds miss, and drones are cheap. X-RATE is enemy value destroyed per dollar you spend — above 1.0 you are winning the war. Top 5 per difficulty post to the shared command net.
THE ENEMY LEARNS On STANDARD and above, raids re-route toward your leaks and away from your kill zones. Down recon SPARROWS before they exfiltrate, or the next raid arrives coordinated. Night and weather degrade optics and lasers — radar does not care.
RAID MODE Flip to ATTACK on the menu: compose the drone strike package yourself and fly it against a defended grid — presets, random grids, or any pasted checkpoint code.
